The Lead Flow Fix helps service businesses tighten the path between online interest and actual next steps. That can include your digital front door, Google presence, WhatsApp flow, saved replies, lead tracking, and the follow-up structure most businesses should have had already.

Problem articulation

This is what the leak usually looks like

  • People hear about you, then struggle to find clear information.
  • They message on Instagram or Facebook, but replies are slow or scattered.
  • The same questions get typed over and over.
  • WhatsApp is being used, but not properly.
  • Nobody knows which lead is still warm and which one is gone.
  • Follow-up depends too much on memory.
  • Business gets lost in the shuffle.

That does not always look dramatic from the outside. It just quietly costs you.

What the offer includes

What gets tightened up

Depending on fit and scope, the Lead Flow Fix can include:

A cleaner digital front door

A clearer place for people to understand what you do, what to ask, and how to move.

Google presence cleanup

Google Business Profile basics, contact details, calls to action, and trust signals.

WhatsApp flow

Clearer entry points, WhatsApp Business optimisation, saved replies, and FAQ logic.

Lead labels and tracking

Simple labels and a lightweight tracker so warm leads do not disappear into memory.

Follow-up structure

Prompts and practical recovery language for people who went quiet but are not necessarily gone.

Usable handover

A setup the business can keep using after the work is technically complete.

Best fit for

Owner-led service businesses already getting some attention.

This is most useful for businesses losing momentum between interest and action.

  • Beauty businesses, clinics, consultants, service providers, event businesses, and conversation-based sales teams.
Probably not for you if
  • You want a giant custom buildout.
  • You need enterprise-level software integration.
  • You are looking for full social media management.
