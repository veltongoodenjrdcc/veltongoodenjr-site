Who I am in plain language

Useful at the intersection of story, structure, and execution

I am a Digital Brand Strategist based in Jamaica, with work that crosses content, digital presence, production, workflow thinking, and delivery. That mix is part of the value, not a side note.

Some opportunities need strategic clarity. Some need a cleaner website, better content, stronger production, or a more reliable system underneath the visible work. I tend to be most helpful where those things overlap.

I care about usefulness, honesty, and work that can actually hold up once the launch energy wears off.

What fits

The opportunities I am open to

Not every enquiry needs the same path, but the good ones usually fit into one of these lanes.

Interested clients

Brand clarity, websites, content support, production, voiceover, or practical digital cleanup for businesses and organisations.

Brand partnerships

Creator or brand-facing opportunities that make sense for my voice, audience, and the way I work. Fit matters more than volume.

Collaborations

Creative, editorial, or production collaborations where the combined output is stronger than either side working alone.

Media and speaking

Panels, workshops, podcasts, interviews, and conversations about digital clarity, content systems, branding, and practical execution.

Aligned roles

Hiring conversations are welcome, but they are part of the mix rather than the centre of the site. The right fit matters more than the title alone.

What tends to be a strong fit

  • Work that values clarity, usefulness, and honest representation.
  • Brands and projects with a real need for thoughtful digital execution, not just more noise.
  • Opportunities where there is room for editorial honesty and clear communication.

What is less likely to fit

  • Opportunities that ask for inflated claims, forced endorsements, or awkward alignment.
  • Projects that need me to pretend to be stronger in an area than I actually am.
  • Anything that clashes with basic integrity, respect, or the kind of work I can stand behind comfortably.
