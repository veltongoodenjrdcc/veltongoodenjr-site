What to include

A useful note answers these quickly.

  • What the opportunity is and who it is for.
  • The brand, organisation, platform, or event behind it.
  • The format: sponsored content, interview, workshop, panel, demo, campaign, or collaboration.
  • Timeline, location or platform, and any budget or range available.
  • Why you think my voice, work, or skill set fits.

Fit filter

The best opportunities have a real reason for the match.

I am a better fit when the collaboration benefits from a systems-minded creative lens, a grounded Caribbean perspective, practical digital brand thinking, or someone who can make a messy idea easier to understand.

  • Strong fit: practical tools, digital platforms, creator systems, business education, service-business support, content, websites, communication, and useful community conversations.
  • Possible fit: podcasts, interviews, panels, campaigns, live appearances, creator-led demos, workshops, editorial collaborations, and brand storytelling.
  • Weak fit: vague exposure-only pitches, rushed scripts with no alignment, unrelated products, or anything that needs me to pretend to care about something I do not.
