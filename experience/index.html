Best fit

Where I tend to operate best

I am strongest in roles and projects where strategy and execution need to stay connected. That could be a Digital Brand Strategist role, content and digital leadership, production-heavy brand support, or a crossover function inside a smaller team.

What usually makes the fit work is not one title. It is the need for someone who can think, make, coordinate, and improve the structure around the work at the same time.
