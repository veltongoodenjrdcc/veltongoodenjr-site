The main problem I solve

A lot of businesses do not have a visibility problem. They have a follow-through problem.

The page looks decent. The Instagram is active enough. Somebody gets interested. Then the trail goes cold. The info is scattered, the path is clunky, the replies are slow, and the warm lead quietly dies.

That is not just annoying. It is expensive.

This is the kind of problem I am increasingly focused on fixing.
