The kinds of opportunities I am open to

Creator-led collaborations

Ideas where the output needs taste, clarity, digital fluency, and a point of view that does not feel copied from a template.

Brand partnerships

Thoughtful collaborations that suit my voice, strengths, audience direction, and actual interests.

Workshops and speaking

Conversations around digital clarity, content systems, communication, websites, or practical brand thinking.

Creative and editorial collaborations

Projects where the combined output is stronger because both sides showed up properly.

What helps

A little context goes a long way.

Tell me what the opportunity is, what stage it is at, what you need from me, and what kind of timeline you are working with.

If it is a collaboration, send the shape of the idea.

If it is a speaking or media request, send the format, date, and audience.

If it is a brand conversation, tell me what the brand is trying to say, who it needs to reach, and why you think my voice or skill set fits.

Resource note
