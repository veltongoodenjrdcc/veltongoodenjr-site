Privacy Notice

Last updated: 2026-04-24

This notice explains how your information is handled when you use the contact form or reach out through this website.

What I collect

When you use the contact form, I collect your name, email address, enquiry type, preferred contact method, and message. Phone and reference link fields are optional.

Why I collect it

Your information is collected only to read and respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to continue a conversation about possible work.

How it is processed

Your message is forwarded to my business email through a Google Apps Script form handler. I do not sell or share your data for marketing purposes.

Storage and retention

Messages are stored in email and retained only as long as reasonably necessary, including where an active engagement or follow-up conversation requires it.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your submitted information, ask that it be corrected, or request deletion by emailing info@veltongoodenjr.com.

Hosting and processors

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Email is handled via Google Workspace. Form submissions are processed via Google Apps Script.

Cookies and tracking

This website does not currently use cookies or third-party analytics scripts.
